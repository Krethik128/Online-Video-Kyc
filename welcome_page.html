What you need before to do for further proceeding


Step 1: Prepare your Physical PAN card, a white paper, and a pen.


Step 2: Grant permissions on your device for camera, location, and microphone. This data will be securely accessed by the bank and will never be made public.


Step 3: Make sure your camera and microphone is working properly


Step 4: Position your face within the frame and ensure it is well-lit. Follow the prompts to capture your face from different angles.


Step 5: Ensure you are in a well-lit room with minimal background noise. Position your device's camera so that your face is clearly visible.
